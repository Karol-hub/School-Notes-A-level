# Surds
$\sqrt{ab} = \sqrt{a} * \sqrt{b}$
$\sqrt{\frac{a}{b}}=\frac{\sqrt{a}}{\sqrt{b}}$
# Rationalising
$\frac{1}{\sqrt{a}} * \frac{\sqrt{a}}{\sqrt{a}}$
Multiply by surd on bottom to get rid of it

$\frac{1}{a-\sqrt{b}} * \frac{a+\sqrt{b}}{a+\sqrt{b}}$
$\frac{1}{a+\sqrt{b}} * \frac{a-\sqrt{b}}{a-\sqrt{b}}$
