- rate of change of velocity 
- vector quantity
- $a =\frac{\Delta v}{\Delta t}$

| Symbol | Unit      | Definition   |
|:------:| --------- | ------------ |
|  $a$   | $ms^{-2}$ | acceleration      |
|  $v$   | $ms^{-1}$       | velocity |
|  $t$   | $s$       | time         |

Uniform acceleration - acceleration is constant
