Rate of flow of charge particles
SI units: $m\,s^{-1}$
$$I=nqvA$$

| Symbol | Unit | Definition |
| ------ | ---- | ---------- |
| $I$    | $A$     | [[Current]]           |
| $n$    | $m^{-3}$     | [[Charge Carrier Density]]           |
| $q$    | $C$     | [[Charge]] of a single charge carrier |
| $v$    | $m\,s^{-1}$     | Drift Velocity |
| $A$       | $m^2$     | Cross Sectional Area |
