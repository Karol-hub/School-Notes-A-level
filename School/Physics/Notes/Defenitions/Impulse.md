Change in [[Momentum]]
$$\Delta p = F \Delta t$$

| Symbol | Unit        | Definition             |
| ------ | ----------- | ---------------------- |
| $p$    | $kgms^{-1}$ | change in [[Momentum]] |
| $F$    | $N$         | [[Force]]              |
| $t$    | $s$         | [[SI Units\|Time]]     |
# Derivation
[[Newton's second law of motion]] states $F =ma$
$$\therefore F = \frac{\Delta mv}{\Delta t} \; \text{since } \; a = \frac{\Delta v}{\Delta t}$$
You can rearrange the equation to get
$$F \Delta t = \Delta (mv)$$
$$F \Delta t = \Delta p$$
