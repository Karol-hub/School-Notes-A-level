Energy per temperature increment per amount of substance (energy per kelvin per mol)
$$R = 8.314 \; JK^{-1}mol^{-1}$$
$$R=N_{A}k$$

| Symbol | Unit              | Definition             |
| ------ | ----------------- | ---------------------- |
| $R$    | $JK^{-1}mol^{-1}$ | Molar gas Constant     |
| $N_A$  | $mol^{-1}$        | [[Avogadro constant]]  |
| $k$    | $JK^{-1}$         | [[Boltzmann constant]] |
