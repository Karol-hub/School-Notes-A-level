The rate of energy transfer
Measured in Watts (W)
# [[Topic 2- Mechanics]]
$$P = \frac{E}{t}$$

| Symbol | Unit          | Definition                   |
| ------ | ------------- | ---------------------------- |
| $P$    | $W$ | Power                        |
| $E$    | $J$           | Energy transferred/work done |
| $t$    | $s$           | [[SI Units\|time]]           |

Can be rearranged into 
$$P *t=E$$
